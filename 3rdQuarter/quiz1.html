Citizen Jake The personal saga of a Filipino and his struggles with the contradictions within his own social class and the demands of a dysfunctional political family in a Third World nation. Writers: Mike De Leon, Atom Araullo, Noel Pascual

Director: Mike De Leon 		This movie tackled the most difficult time of the Philippine Political history. It was aired at a time when we need more light as we are approaching darkness once again.
- danieljacobs-18980
Parasite Greed and class discrimination threaten the newly-formed symbiotic relationship between the wealthy Park family and the destitute Kim clan. Casts: Bong Joon Ho, Han Jin-won

Director: Bong Joon Ho 		I can't remember the last time I saw a movie that contained as many genres as 'Parasite'. The movie starts out almost like an 'Ocean's Eleven' heist film and then expands into a comedy, mystery, thriller, drama, romance, crime and even horror film. It really did have everything and it was strikingly good at all of them too.
- jtindahouse
Give Up Tomorrow When a teenager from a political family in the Philippines is accused of a double murder, the country's entire judicial system is put to the test after years of alleged corruption. Writers: Michael Collins, Eric Daniel Metzgar, Marty Syjuco

Director: Michael Collins 		I saw this film two years ago as a secret screening at the True/False Film Festival. It was easily my favorite documentary that year, and one which will leave you disturbed long after the credits have rolled.
